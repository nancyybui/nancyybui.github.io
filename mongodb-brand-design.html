MongoDB Brand Design

I drive company spirit across global teams by producing a variety of visual brand assets, creating strategic iconography. Spearheaded an effort to simplify the designer-to-developer workflow by identifying pain points in brand collateral production.

Overview

This past summer, I was given the incredible opportunity to intern for MongoDB’s Brand Experience Team. Over a course of  ten weeks, I learned the importance of building a successful brand that translates across company culture and the customer experience.

My work has continued throughout the year as I am currently working on their team as a Brand Design Contractor. Fun fact: I was the first MongoDB intern based in Palo Alto!
