Branding a Career Fair

Overview
Design at UCSD launched an initiative this past summer to host UC San Diego’s first design-centric career fair. I crafted a new brand identity to capture the spirit of the event, while upholding the core values and brand identity of Design at UCSD. The promotional material I created includes an event website, tabloids and flyers, marketing collateral, sticker sheets, t-shirts, and more.

Brand Ideation + Creation
To inform my brand decisions, I chatted with Design at UCSD members to gain a better understanding of their perception of the event and what the brand experience should be like. After several discussions with the leadership team, we decided that the brand should convey the skills of our design students, encourage employers to start a conversation with students and overall showcase the diversity in our community. Our final brand exudes a humanistic and friendly feel, with characters striding towards their dream design careers. Small motifs such as sticky notes and pencils were incorporated to visualize the nature of design.
