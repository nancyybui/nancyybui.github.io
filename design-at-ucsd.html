Marketing Collateral

During the spring, I planned and led a workshop on color and typography with the support of my team. My co-chair and I taught the design community basic visual, brand, and graphic design principles to keep in mind throughout their interaction design projects. From creating the materials to present, to the activities that the students engaged in, we helped increase the visual design interest and engagement in the UC San Diego design community.

The material spanned from the different types of color palettes and the feelings they portray, to the different types of fonts and the ways that they can be utilized. This was all tied back to brand design, and why it’s important in any design project.
