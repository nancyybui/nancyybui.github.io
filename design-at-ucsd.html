Growing a Design Community

I've been fortunate enough to co-lead and grow the undergraduate design community at UC San Diego through Design at UCSD. I also lead the creative direction, visual design, and branding of all initiatives.

What is Design at UCSD?

"Design at UCSD is a pre-professional organization that aims to be at the forefront of design thinking at UC San Diego. Through hosting events, talks, and interactive workshops, our hope is to bridge the gap between design academia and industry while equipping members with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the rapidly evolving and innovative field of human-centered design."

Color + Typography

During the spring, I planned and led a workshop on color and typography. My co-chair and I taught basic visual, brand, and graphic design principles to keep in mind when designing interaction design projects. From creating the materials to present to the activities that the students engaged in, we helped increase the visual design interest and engagement in the UC San Diego design community.
